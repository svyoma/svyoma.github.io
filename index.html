About this web space

This is a space where I put digitized Jaina literature in Saṁskṛta, Prākṛta and Apabhraṃśa. The portal aims to preserve and promote our linguistic heritage through accessible digital formats.

Browse through the various sections to explore ancient texts, daily verses, and scholarly resources related to Jaina literature.
