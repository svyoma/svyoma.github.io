      • Vīrastutidvātriṃśikā by disciple of Ratnākarasūri

        32 verses in praise of Mahāvīrasvāmī depicting the five kalyāṇakas and major events of his life in Indravajrā.

          • Siddhacaturviṃśikā by Ānandaghana

            11 verses in praise of Mahāvīrasvāmī using technical grammatical terms with commentary by Bhāvaprabhasūri
